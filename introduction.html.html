How to Use

Step 1: Edit the price of the raw materials and the

environmental factors according to your location.

Step 2: Select the type of concrete structure

you want to calculate (Slab, Beam, Column, or Footing).
Step 3: Enter the dimensions of your structure in your preferred units
Step 4: Choose the desired concrete strength (PSI) based on your project requirements.
Step 5: Click "Calculate" to get the required quantities and costs.
Step 6: Save your calculation with a project name for future reference.

Physical Mixing

Step 1

Preparation: Ensure all materials are clean and free from contaminants.

Step 2

Dry Mixing: Mix cement and sand thoroughly in dry condition.

Step 3

Creater or Well Create a crater or well on the center of the mixture.

Step 4

Gravel Addition Add gravel to the mixture evenly

Step 5

Water Addition: Add water on the creater/well look-like or in the center of the sand and cement mixture.

Step 6

Mixing Time: Mix until the required consistency is achieved.

Step 7

Consistency Check: Apply the solution to the target reinforced concrete framework.

Step 8

Curing Process: Allow the concrete to cure properly for optimal strength.
